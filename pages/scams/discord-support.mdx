# Discord Support

## How it works
1. Scammers create fake Discord support accounts
2. They reach out to you claiming to provide assistance or that they reported you
3. They trick you into revealing personal information or installing malware

## I've already fallen for this scam
1. Contact Discord support immediately.
2. Change your account password.
3. Run a malware scan on your device.

## How do I avoid this scam?
- Be cautious of unsolicited messages from supposed Discord support
- Verify the legitimacy of the support account through official channels
- Never share personal information or download files from untrusted sources
