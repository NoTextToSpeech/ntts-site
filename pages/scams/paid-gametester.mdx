# Paid Game Tester

## How it works
1. You are contacted by a scammer who claims to be a game developer or a game testing company
2. They offer you to be a game tester and payment
3. They send a game for you to play and test
4. You play the game and provide feedback

## I've already fallen for this scam
1. Uninstall the game immediately
2. Do not provide any personal information
3. Block the scammer

## How do I avoid this scam?
- Do not accept offers from strangers
- Do not download games from untrusted sources
- Do not provide personal information to strangers
